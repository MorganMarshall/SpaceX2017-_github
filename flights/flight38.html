Flight No.38 : July 5, 2017

Flight 38:

Due to the constraints of sending a heavy satellite (6,761 kg) to GTO, the rocket flew in its expendable configuration and the first-stage booster was not recovered. The rocket achieved a super-synchronous orbit peaking at 43,000 km (27,000 mi), exceeding minimum requirements of 28,000 km (17,000 mi).
