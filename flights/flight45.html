Flight No.45 : December 15, 2017

Flight 45:

The first launch to take place at Pad 40 after the Amos-6 explosion in 2016. Second reuse of a Dragon capsule, previously flown on CRS-6, and fourth reuse of a booster, previously flown on CRS-11, making this flight the first time both major components were reused at the same time; this flight had the 20th successful booster landing. -wiki
